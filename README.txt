Travel with Nat — Modern V5
---------------------------------
How to run locally:
  python3 -m http.server 8000
Then open:
  http://localhost:8000/TravelWithNat_Modern_V5/

What to do next:
- Put your images into /img/:
    - img/profile.png (logo)
    - img/swimming-pool.jpg (hero background)
    - img/preview.jpg (social share image for OG/Twitter cards)
- Replace the iframe src in index.html (#quote section) with your Cognito Forms embed URL.

Includes:
- Centered cookie popup (fade+zoom, Read More opens modal, 1s delay)
- Centered modals (Privacy & Cookies) with frosted overlay
- Stardust cursor trail with toggle
- Back-to-top button (⤴) with smooth scroll
- Smooth scrolling for CTA and footer Get a Quote
- SEO & Open Graph meta tags
- Poppins font, gradients, rounded corners
