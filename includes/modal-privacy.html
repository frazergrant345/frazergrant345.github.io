1. Introduction

Welcome to Travel with Nat. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or contact us for travel quotes.

2. Who We Are

Travel with Nat is an independent travel service based in the United Kingdom. For privacy queries, contact info@travelwithnat.co.uk.

3. Information We Collect

  • Name, email, phone
  • Travel preferences/details for quotes

We may also collect limited technical data (e.g., IP, browser) to improve the site.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to enquiries and provide quotes
  • Manage bookings and follow-ups
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal/regulatory requirements

We do not sell or share your information for marketing.

5. Third-Party Services

Our contact form is powered by Cognito Forms, which securely processes and stores submitted data on our behalf.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary for the purpose collected or as required by law.

7. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you can access, correct or delete your data, withdraw consent, and complain to the ICO.

8. Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss or misuse.

9. Updates

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
