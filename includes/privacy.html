Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Travel with Nat. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or contact us for travel quotes.

2. Who We Are

Travel with Nat is an independent travel service based in the United Kingdom.
For any privacy-related queries, please contact us at: info@travelwithnat.co.uk.

3. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide through our contact form or other means, such as:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Travel preferences or details for quotes
  • Any other information you provide in your message

We may also collect limited technical information automatically (such as browser type, IP address, and general usage data) to help improve our website.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to enquiries and provide travel quotes
  • Manage and process bookings or follow-up communication
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal or regulatory requirements

We do not sell or share your information for marketing purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

Our contact form is powered by Cognito Forms, which securely processes and stores submitted data on our behalf.
View their privacy policy at https://www.cognitoforms.com/privacy.

6. Data Retention

We only keep your information as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry or fulfil a booking, unless required by law to retain it longer.

7. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for processing
  • Complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO)

8. Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

9. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
